Summarizes the analysis

We examined the academic performance of different schools ( Charter school vs District school) based on various metrics such as average math and reading scores, as well as the percentage of students passing math, reading, and overall.
The dataframe provided the information for each school, including its type, size, busget, and academic performance.


Conclusions and Comparisons

There's a good differences in average scores between different school types.

Charter schools tend to have higher overall passing percentage.  May suggest that the charter school could be implementing a better teaching methods. 
Charter schools shows higher math and reading scores on average compares to District school.
